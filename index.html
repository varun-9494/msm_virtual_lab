Course Objectives

What You Will Learn

Aligned to JNTUH R25 course outcomes for Material Science & Metallurgy

Crystal Structures

Understand metallic crystal structures, imperfections and strengthening mechanisms in relation to material behaviour.

Phase Diagrams

Interpret binary phase diagrams and the Fe–Fe₃C diagram to explain microstructural transformations in alloys.

Heat Treatment

Apply different heat treatment processes and use TTT/CCT diagrams to predict microstructures and properties.

Engineering Alloys

Explain properties and applications of alloy steels, cast irons and non-ferrous alloys for engineering use.

Advanced Materials

Apply knowledge of ceramics, composites, polymers and advanced materials for engineering material selection.
